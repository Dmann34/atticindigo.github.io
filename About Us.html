About Us

Attic Indigo was born from the desire to help women break free of society's rules surrounding femininity. We embrace the idea that every woman possesses a unique quality which contributes to her magic within. We seek to inspire women from all walks of life to be wild, loud and radical. Here, you will find tools to help you step into your power and unveil the mystery of your inner goddess.
